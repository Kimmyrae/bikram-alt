About

  • About

    Bikram Yoga is the 26 postures sequence selected and developed by Bikram Choudhury. It is the original hot yoga, practiced in a room heated to 105 degrees Fahrenheit.

  • Instructors

    Bikram Choudhury, the creator of Bikram Yoga, personally trains and certifies his instructors to ensure that his methods and philosophy are preserved and properly taught.

  • Research

    PURE Action is devoted to establishing the feasibility and the effectiveness of yoga therapy as an alternative medicine through continued research here.
